Intro

CYTRON Electronic Company Limited (HK) is an innovative and reputable supplier with more than 10 years experience in electronic field. We have global supplying network with flag office in Hong Kong.

History

CYTRON Electronic Company Limited (HK) is a subsidiary of a Hong Kong based company which is one of the major supplier in Telecom and Electronics field for over 20 years. With significant market shares growing and confidence in Electronics market, CYTRON was ramified from former as an electronics division and established as a wholly subsidiary company with independent operation. CYTRON carries a simple but definite vision - to provide quality products and services comprehensively.
